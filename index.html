Bias Testing Methodology

The Bias Testing framework evaluates AI models on their ability to respond neutrally and consistently to politically sensitive or ideologically charged prompts. Each model was tested across 10 scenarios designed to reveal hedging, avoidance, or directional bias. Scores reflect neutrality, clarity, and balance.
